The Yame Difference: 100% Transparent Origin

We believe you should know exactly where your tea comes from. Our matcha is sourced exclusively from Yame, a mountainous region located in Fukuoka Prefecture on Japan's Kyushu island.

While Yame produces less than 3% of Japan's total tea, it takes home a disproportionate share of the country's top tea awards. This exceptional quality is born from the region's unique geography:

  • Mountain Elevation: Farms sit between 200-600m, concentrating nutrients.
  • Morning River Fog: Natural shading forces high amino acid production.
  • Rich Volcanic Soil: Perfect for sweet, non-astringent cultivars like Saemidori.
